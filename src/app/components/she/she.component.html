(S)he

Aparna Agarwal and Rohit Suratekar
While trying out different ways of making comic strips on various topics, I decided to try my hands at something new! Rather than comedizing a story, I chose a poem instead. I had initially thought of working on my own old poems but later I decided to use a third-party poem to give myself some room for new poetic scenarios in the process. Fortunately, I am surrounded by a host of talented artists, including poets. My Ph.D. batchmate, Aparna, is a fantastic poet and had earlier sent me some of her works for a read. I chanced upon this fortuitous opportunity and went on to comedize one of her poetic works, with her due permission. The poem used in this comic is copyright of Ms. Aparna Agarwal. However, the comic interpretation is my intellectual property. I hope my conception of Aparna's poem has been able to recapitulate some parts of the original poem :D Such was the beauty of this exercise, that the same piece of art is perceived differently by different artists. I have added a lot of small details and artistic arrangement of elements to convey my emotions and perspectives.
Enjoy the beautiful poem :)
