Chronicles of the GreatFool Soul

Rohit Suratekar
I defended my PhD thesis at National Centre for Biological Sciences on 29th October 2018. One of the most important parts of my thesis was the 'Acknowledgements'. I was working in this institute for almost 6 years and this long journey would not have been possible without help and support from multiple people. Generally one can express their sentiments through words or pictures. However, I decided to acknowledge them in my own style. I converted my acknowledgements into a comic strip. Every panel is carefully designed to convey my memories and sentiments. You won't be able to understand many of these unless you know the characters, my apologies for that. However, I want to cherish these memories (out of millions) the most. You can download this comic by clicking on following buttons.
