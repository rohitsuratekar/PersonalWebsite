Project Campus Demo 1.0

‘Project Campus’ was started in 2009. It was time when Flash had recently purchased by Adobe from Macromedia Inc. There was hype of small flash games. After looking at click based flash games, I decided to make my own game. I made this game entirely from scratch in ActionScript 3.0. I used our college hostel map for making game this world. I never completed this game. Here I am uploading whatever beta version I completed. This is now completely outdated technology and I am keeping here for historical purpose. After this game, I implemented flash snippets in many places. One of the Online Treasure Hunt I had made for our college technical fest was pretty famous.
