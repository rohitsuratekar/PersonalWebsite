How it all started ?
I have been always attracted towards visual art. I used to click rare photographs by my father’s old Kodak vintage camera on some special occasion. That time, it was just fascination towards fancy technology. Soon there was advent of digital cameras and everyone was creating memories of their own with their camera. My first camera was Sony Cyber-shot. My father had gifted it to me during my graduation year. However, my actual photography experience started when I purchased by own SLR in 2014. It was Canon 600D with 2 kit lenses. I got it for cheap from some stock clearance sale. Soon I become ‘Amateur Photographer’. You can check my photo gallery for few samples. I have also done some videography.
What do I have ?
Canon 600D, Canon EF-S 18-55mm f/3.5-5.6 IS II, Canon EF-S 55 - 250 mm f/4-5.6 IS II, Yongnuo 50mm f/1.8, Canon EF 100mm f/2.8L Macro, Vanguard CX203AGH Espod, Few triggers and Filters
What do I like ?
I generally like experimenting with different styles of photography. So far I have tried plenty of different types. My personal favorite is 'Macro Photography'.
