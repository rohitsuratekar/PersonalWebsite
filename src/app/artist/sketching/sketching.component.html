Background

I started drawing very young. I use to draw on blank space on newspaper and color empty spaces. My mother was very encouraging and provided me all tools needed to develop drawing skills. She herself was good painter. Hence, I improved my drawing skills faster than other children at my age. Participation in various drawing exams also boosted my formal knowledge about drawing. However, I never took it as serious hobby. This resulted into average sketching skills. I still actively practice sketching in my free time. I generally do black and white pencil sketching. I like sketching with normal pencils and sometimes ball pen. Now a day I am practicing comic art.
